To process:

  1. Click on the Refresh Status Information icon.
  2. Double click on the Renewal Denied Items - Notified queue.
    1. This opens the General Search - Borrowing window.
  4. Double click on a request from the list to open it.
    1. This opens the General Update Form window.
  6. If the Due Date is today or earlier...
    1. Click on the Email menu item.
      1. Click on the Denied Renewal Overdue submenu item.
        1. This opens the Route Email Form window.
        2. Edit the email as needed.
      3. Click on the Send button.
      4. The Email is sent.
      5. The request is updated to Renewal Denied Items - Overdue status.
  8. Close the request.
  9. Repeat until all requests are processed.
