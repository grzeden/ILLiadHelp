Received Partial-Incorrect Item

Dennis Grzegorzewski, 18 May 2015 (created 18 May 2015).

UNFINISHED.

Description:

Requests in this status need to be resent from the lender. An e-mail to the lender needs to be sent. This status is not common because it is an intermediate status to 'Emailed to Resend Partial/Incorrect Item'.

To process:

  1. Click on the Refresh Status Information icon.
  2. Double click on Received Partial/Incorrect Item queue.
    1. This opens the General Search - Borrowing window.
  3. Double click on a request from the list to open it.
    1. This opens the General Update Form window.
  4. Click on the [...] button in the Lender field.
  5. Copy the lenders EMail address.
  6. If there is no lender information or if it is incomplete...
  7. Click on the PD button and fill out the form with information from the Policies Directory.
  8. Close the Lender Address window.
  9. Click on the EMail menu item.
  10. Click on the Resend Request submenu item.
  11. Paste the lenders EMail address in to To: field of the Email.
  12. Click on the Send button.
  13. The window closes and the status is changed to Emailed to Resend Partial/Incorrect Item.
  14. Add a note that reads Emailed to resend.
  15. Close the General Update Form window.
  16. Repeat until all items are processed.
