Awaiting Recalled Processing

Dennis Grzegorzewski, 24 November 2014 (created 3 November 2014).

UNFINISHED.

Description:

This queue holds requests for items that have been recalled by their lending libraries. These items need to be returned immediately, and are processed with greater urgency than overdue items. Recalled items must be returned in three days. There are NO EXCEPTIONS.

To process:

  1. Click on the Refresh (F5) icon.
  2. Click on the Main (F12) tab
  3. Double click on the Awaiting Recalled Processing queue.
    1. This opens the Awaiting Recalled Processing page.
  4. Double click on a request from the list to open it.
    1. This opens a Request window.
  5. Click on the Send E-Mail button.
    1. Click on the Recall Notice submenu item.
    2. This opens the Edit Email Form page.
      1. Edit the email as needed.
      2. Click on the Send button.
    3. The Email is sent.
    4. The request is updated to Recalled Items â€“ Notified status.
  6. Close the request.
  7. Repeat until all requests are processed.
