Cancelled by Customer

Dennis Grzegorzewski, 18 May 2015 (created 18 May 2015).

UNFINISHED.

Description:

Requests are placed in this queue by the customer who desires to cancel their request. We need to finish the cancellation so their request is finished.

To process:

  1. If in the OCLC Information, the ILL Number, and Lending String are blank...
    1. Click on Cancel Request button in the Next Step ribbon section.
    2. Select Cancelled by Patron from the Reasons for Cancellation drop-down menu.
    3. Uncheck the Send E-mail check-box.
      1. So no email is sent.
    4. Click on the Cancel Request button.
      1. This will route the request to the Cancelled By ILL Staff queue.
  2. Otherwise...
    1. Route the request back to Request Sent.
      1. Only requests that have not been sent can be cancelled by the user.
  3. Repeat until all requests are processed.
