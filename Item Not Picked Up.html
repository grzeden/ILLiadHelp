Item Not Picked Up

Dennis Grzegorzewski, 24 November 2014 (created 3 November 2014).

UNFINISHED.

Description:

This queue temporarily holds requests for items that are pulled from the hold shelf and Checked In at the circulation desk, because the were not picked up before their due date.

These requests should automatically continue on to the Awaiting Return Label Printing status.

To process:

Route this request to Awaiting Extensive Searching, with a note, for futher processing.
