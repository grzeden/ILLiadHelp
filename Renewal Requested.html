Renewal Requested

Dennis Grzegorzewski, 24 November 2014 (created 3 November 2014).

UNFINISHED.

Description:

This queue contains requests where the customer has requested a renewal. Before these are sent to the lender we need to update their DUE DATE. This is the new date we are asking to keep the item until. The standard change to the DUE DATE is exactly one month from today. Generally lenders renew one time and for one month.

To process:

  1. Click on the Refresh (F5) icon.
    1. This refreshes the listed data to the current state.
  3. Click on the Main (F12) tab.
  4. Click on the Borrowing tab.
    1. This displays the list of Borrowing request queues.
  6. Double click on the Renewal Requested queue.
    1. This opens the Renewal Requested page.
  8. Double click on a request from the list to open it.
    1. This opens a Borrowing Request window.
  10. If the OCLC Status in the OCLC Information section is RECIEVED or RENEWAL_OK.
    1. Set the Due Date in the Item Information section to one month from today.
    2. Click on the Save Changes icon.
    3. Close the Borrowing Request window.
  12. If the OCLC Status in the OCLC Information section is NO_RENEWAL or RECALLED.
    1. Uncheck the Allow Renewals? check box.
    2. Route the request to Request Sent.
  14. Continue until all requests in the Renewal Requested queue are processed.
  15. Click on the Borrowing ribbon tab.
  16. Click on the Send OCLC Renewal Requests icon in the Processing section.
  17. A Renewals Processed dialog window will open, and display the number of records sent to table for OCLC Updating, which is how many renewals are being requested.
  18. Click on the OK button.
