Checked Out to Customer

Dennis Grzegorzewski, 18 May 2015 (created 18 May 2015).

UNFINISHED.

Description:

This status holds requests that have been checked out to the customer. They remain at this status until they are checked in at the Circulation desk.

To more efficiently manage the renewal process, we place renewals for requests 8 days before their due date in order to have a renewal in process before the renewal reminder email is sent 7 days before the due date. The reminder email is now a fall back in case the renewal requests are not placed.

The reasoning behind placing renewals for all requests is to avoid all the time spent reminding the customer to place a renewal for the item. Essentially patrons will receive notices of new renewed dates until the request is denied renewal and must be returned.

To process:

  1. The columns to take note of are.
    1. Due Date.
      1. Sort ascending by this column.
    2. Last Overdue Notice Sent.
      1. Customers accounts are Blocked on the third email sent out 2 weeks after the due date.
    3. Cleared.
      1. Customers who are Blocked will have a B in this column.
