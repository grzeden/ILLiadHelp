Awaiting Galvin Stacks Search

Dennis Grzegorzewski, 24 November 2014 (created 3 November 2014).

DEPRICATED.

Description:

This status holds student requests for items that our catalog suggests we may own. It is necessary to check our stacks to determine if the item is actually available.

To do this we must print out search slips and take them to the stacks to physically find the item. If the requested item is found on the shelf the request is cancelled. If the requested item is not found, the request is routed to the Awaiting Request Processing (NOS) queue for further processing.

To process:

  1. Double click on the Awaiting Galvin Stacks Search queue.
    1. This opens the Awaiting Galvin Stacks Search page.
  2. Click on any request to select it.
  3. Click on the Print dropdown menu to open it.
  4. Click on the Clear Print Queue dropdown menu item.
  5. Click on the OK button to close the popup dialogue.
  6. Click on the Print dropdown menu to open it again.
  7. Click on the Print All Requests dropdown menu item.
  8. Click on the OK button to close the popup dialogue.
  9. Double click on the Borrowing Search Slips Word document shortcut on the desktop to open it.
  10. Click Yes on the dialog to allow word to make changes.
  11. Click Yes on the dialog to load the data into the Word document.
  12. Click on the Mailings ribbon tab.
  13. Click on the Finish & Merge buttom to open its dropdown menu.
  14. Click on the Print Documents... menu item.
  15. Select the All option is on the Merge to Printer dialog, and click the OK button.
  16. Click the OK button on the Print dialog to print the search slips.
  17. Cut the sheets in the middle to seperate them.
  18. Follow the instructions on the search slips to process.
