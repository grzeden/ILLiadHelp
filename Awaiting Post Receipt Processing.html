Awaiting Post Receipt Processing

Dennis Grzegorzewski, 24 November 2014 (created 3 November 2014).

UNFINISHED.

Description:

Requests are automatically routed to this queue after being processed in the Check Item In From Lending Library page. This process is used to print the book bands for the items in the Awaiting Post Receipt Processing queue. The result of this process is to change the status of these received items to Awaiting Customer Contact in ILLiad, and to have them shelved on the Circulation Hold Shelf.

To process:

  1. Click on the Refresh (F5) icon.
  2. Click on the Main (F12) tab
  3. Click the Borrowing ribbon tab.
    1. This opens the Borrowing ribbon.
  4. Click the Print Receives button.
    1. This opens an ILLiad Printer Settings (BorrowingLoanSlips.doc) dialog box.
  5. Select ILL Printer from the list.
  6. Click on the Print button in the dialog box.
    1. This will open two Word documents.
    2. Select the Form Letters1 Word document.
    3. Click the Quick Print (ILL Printer) icon.
      1. This prints out the book band slips.
  7. Close both documents without saving.
  8. Cut up the book band slips.
  9. Carefully match each book band slip to an item.
  10. Affix white book bands along with the printouts to each item.
    1. Do not tape anything to the item being borrrowed.
    2. Do not tape to the lenders paperwork.
    3. Place all loose paperwork under our book band.
  11. Shelve all the banded items by patron number on the Hold Shelf at the Circulation Desk.
  12. Move on to Contact Customers.
