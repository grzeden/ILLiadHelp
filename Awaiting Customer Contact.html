Awaiting Customer Contact

Dennis Grzegorzewski, 24 November 2014 (created 3 November 2014).

UNFINISHED.

Description:

Requests are automatically routed to this queue after being processed by the Print Receives Borrowing ribbon button process. The result of this process is to change the status of these received items to Customer Notified via E-Mail in ILLiad.

To process:

  1. Click the Borrowing ribbon tab.
    1. This opens the Borrowing ribbon menu.
  2. Click the Contact Customers button in the Receive Requests section of the menu.
    1. This opens the Borrowing Customer Contact page.
  3. Click on the Start Automatic E-Mail button in the Customer Contact ribbon menu.
    1. This will notify customers via e-mail that they have items to pick up on the Circulation Hold Shelf.
    2. The status of these requests will be changed to Customer Notified Via E-mail.
  4. Close the Borrowing Customer Contact page tab.
    1. This finishes the returnable receiving process.

More information:

This information has been updated for ILLiad 8.5.2.0.
