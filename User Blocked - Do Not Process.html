User Blocked - Do Not Process

Dennis Grzegorzewski, 18 May 2015 (created 18 May 2015).

UNFINISHED.

Description:

Requests in this status queue have been submitted by users who have been blocked in ILLiad. This could be the result of graduation or inactivity. It could also be the result of overdue items. After residing in this queue for seven days, if the patron has not taken action to become an active student, obtained alumni privileges, or return overdue items, their requests are deleted.

To process:

DO NOT ROUTE REQUESTS FROM THIS QUEUE

  1. To process simply open and close each request.
    1. If the user has been cleared, requests will automatically be routed...
      • to the Awaiting User Expiration Updating status queue if user has been cleared, but the user's expiration date has passed,
      • or to the Awaiting Citation Completion status queue if user has been cleared, and the user's expiration date has not passed.
  2. Requests remain in this queue until the patron is cleared, or for seven days.

STAFF PROCESS

  1. When the date on the note to delete has past,
    1. If the patron is an Alumni
      1. Click on the Send E-Mail button on the Borrowing Processing ribbon.
      2. Select the No Privileges (Alumni) dropdown menu item.
      3. Click on the Send button on the Email ribbon to finish.
        1. An email is sent to the patron and the request is routed to the Cancelled by ILL Staff status queue.
    2. If the patron is not an Alumni
      1. Click on the Send E-Mail button on the Borrowing Processing ribbon.
      2. Select the Expired Privileges dropdown menu item.
      3. Click on the Send button on the Email ribbon to finish.
        1. An email is sent to the patron and the request is routed to the Cancelled by ILL Staff status queue.
